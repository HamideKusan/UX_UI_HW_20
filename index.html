NASA Responsive Redesign Case Study

National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) is an independent agency of the U.S. federal government responsible for the civilian space program, as well as aeronautics and space research. NASA UX/UI is a complex redesign project consists of two parts. First part is a website design and the second part is an app design. The main idea is to create the most convenient way for educators and students to navigate the cluttered site the best way possible in order to avoid spending more time and getting overwhelmed.

Overview

The Problem

Although NASA is a great source for both outer space and gives reasoning solutions for world crises, the site navigation is overcrowded and cluttered for especially people like busy educators and younger generations who are exposed to eye-pleasing contents all over the social media.

The Possible Solution

The navigation of NASA can be reorganized with implementing the UI designs to enhance usability while maintaining the minimal composition of the original webpage.
